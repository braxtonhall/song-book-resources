I felt the never-dying worm of guilt alive inside me
I had been the author of evil no words could ever describe
And more so much more I feared was yet behind
Sleep fled from my eyes

I wandered like a spirit through the night
And in the light of all my crimes
The shadow of the beast filled my mind

So once again I shunned the face of my fellow man

And left my home in search of solitude
To the mountains that had once sheltered me from the world
But there was always scope for fear and paranoia in my mind
For as long as anything I loved remained behind

Then suddenly I beheld a sight vile and abhorred

Advancing across the mountainside toward me
The murderous wretch beast of my nightmares
Burning with rage I exclaimed

Devil! How dare you approach me?
Do you not fear the wrath of your creator?
Begone! Or feel my vengeance wreaked upon your head!

Fear had fled from my mind

I chased the demon through the night
With murder in my eyes
I closed on him and raised the knife
But when I struck he easily stopped me

And overpowered me in an instant
My life was in his hands when he spoke to me
