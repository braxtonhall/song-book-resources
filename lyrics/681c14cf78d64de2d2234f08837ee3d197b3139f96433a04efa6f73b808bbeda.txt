From my celestial observatory I

gaze upon a cold downtrodden world
A world once ripe with innocence
Now torn asunder by ignorance
When life began fire graced the sky
Casting a light met with a harsh reply
Swallowed by darkness
Man was enslaved
As marks of war were forever engraved

A failing design blood of the divine
Could not be foreseen by my immaculacy
Without light to shine opposing forces entwine
Forever silencing the sound of harmony

An innocent race of men is now bereft

Of its serenity and forced to accept
Perpetual war between darkness and light
Afflicting them all with incurable blight

A maelstrom

Unfolds in a quest for power
Supremacy in every form is sent to devour
Social political
Religious or racial
Regardless of the case
My creation grows pale

The raining debris of a world so cold

Confounds its leaders as it souls are sold
The belligerent fight and the pacifists pray
But neither of them can keep conflict at bay
Now I bear witness to the changing of seasons
An endless cycle without rhyme or reason
Fortune's departure parallels its arrival
And humanity remains in denial
