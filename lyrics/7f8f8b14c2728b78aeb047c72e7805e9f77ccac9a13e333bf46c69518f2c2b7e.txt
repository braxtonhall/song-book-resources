In the shades of the verdant forest
Tender and mild winds are washing the leaves

Echoing whispers lead me to valley
Where a tree stands in a silver dress
Leaves shaped of tears

Of sorrow and lonely years
Gleaming in light of the rising sun
The silvery leaves

Are covering all my tears
As I sit under the willow of tears

Whispers are calling in beaming dreamy air
On the meadow where the willow and I

Wait for the mist to come to dance on silver pond
It slowly guides us to eternal sleep
